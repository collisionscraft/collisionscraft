Desk Show was a collection of objects installed on an old desk found on the third froor of 2031 Studios in Baltimore, MD. Judging from the artifacts left in the space, 2031 Maryland Avenue once housed a real estate investing program, a Christian ministry, and an employment training program prior to being rented as studio space. In April of 2020, we asked 7 artists to each mail us an artwork to be installed on the desk. The work was documented through photos and a 3D model of the space was then sewn together using photogrammetry. There was no physical opening.

Artists: Amy Stober, Audrey Gair, Carolyn Conn, Daniella Ellinger, Garrett Lockhart, Nick Strobelt, Shannon Kelley
