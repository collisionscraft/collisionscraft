Dewey's Revenge Watch This Bumper Crumple

Dewey's Revenge was a one-night-only group show featuring works by Max Anderson, Lulou Margarine, Umico Niwa, and Tessa Paul. The artwork was installed on the first-level of 2518 Guilford Avenue, a Charles Village rowhome that Matt and Korey were subletting for the month of July 2019, while in-between their leases.
