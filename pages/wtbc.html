Dewey's Revenge Watch This Bumper Crumple

Watch This Bumper Crumple was a nomadic group show installed in a 2001 Honda CR-V for one month, from April 27, 2019 to May 27, 2019.
Below are installation shots from the opening night that took place at Round Falls in Baltimore, MD.
