Splat in the Verd Dewey's Revenge Watch This Bumper Crumple

Splat in the Verd was a group show created in collaboration with Sinkhole Project that took place on January 26th, 2020 on a fence overlooking a train in Charles Village, Baltimore. Included are works by Ralph Delia, Esther Hwang, Zack Parker, Kasey Rosen, Duncan Rutstein, and Nicholas Verstraeten.
